welcome

Wabweb is all about you and the web.
Designed to help webdevellopers and web enthusiasts, Wabweb gathers a powerfull toolbox and the most acurate linking tool, that will forward you to the best websites concerning the new internet issues. We have tested and used all links we provide. Our goal is not to reinvent the wheel, but as we got here we faced a whole lot of issues and problems, and we want to share the solutions with you. Please visit the ABOUT US page, and feel free to contact us.
